The Instructor

My Story

I found breathwork out of necessity. After years in high-pressure environments, the physical and mental toll demanded a more sustainable approach to leadership and performance. I didn't want more wellness fluff; I wanted functional tools.

Drawing from a decade of global practice and deeply rooted in Latin American traditions, my protocol is designed for those who command the breath to command their state. It's about regulating the nervous system to optimize cognitive performance under pressure.

Whether working with HR directors, leadership teams, or individual practitioners, my focus remains the same: absolute clarity and real, measurable results. We engineer resilience so you can operate at your highest potential.
